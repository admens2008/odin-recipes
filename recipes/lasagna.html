Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish from Italy that originated in Sicily. It is a type of pasta, and is typically made with a rich tomato sauce and a layer of mozzarella cheese.

This particular Lasagna special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound anchovies
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine sausage, anchovies, garlic, ground beef, red pepper, onion powder, and black pepper.
  2. Mix together water, milk, and sugar; set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat butter until melted.
  4. Add flour and stir until it becomes bubbly.
  5. Gradually add milk mixture, stirring constantly until thickens.
  6. Stir in egg, and heat through.
  7. Remove sauce from heat.
  8. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  9. Place lasagna in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with sauce mixture.
  10. Bake in preheated oven until bubbly and golden brown, about 45 minutes.
  11. Remove lasagna from oven and let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
